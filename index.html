TOWPEP Questionnaire

Self-Assessment ‚Ä¢ Wellness ‚Ä¢ Effectiveness

Activity Description

TOWPEP stands for Transcript of Well-being and Personal Effectiveness. This assessment was designed to measure how students perceive their personal growth, effectiveness, and wellness. I was asked to rate myself from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest) in different areas such as honesty, open-mindedness, adaptability, gratitude, perseverance, relationships, and responsibility. I gave myself high ratings across all items, particularly in being open to new ideas, truthful, hardworking, respectful, adaptable, and consistent.

Reflection

Completing the TOWPEP Questionnaire made me realize the importance of self-assessment and self-awareness. By rating myself, I was able to see how I value qualities like honesty, perseverance, gratitude, and responsibility. I noticed that I often strive to give my best in every task, show respect to others, and remain truthful even in difficult situations.

This reflection gave me a sense of pride because it showed that I am aware of my personal strengths. At the same time, it reminded me that there is always room for growth. For example, while I scored myself high in adaptability and perseverance, I know that real-life challenges may test these traits, and I must continue to practice them in different situations.

Another insight I gained is that personal effectiveness is not only about academic performance but also about character and attitude. It is about how I treat others, how I manage challenges, and how I stay true to my values. This activity encouraged me to keep nurturing these qualities so I can be more resilient, compassionate, and effective both in school and in life.
